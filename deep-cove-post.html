Description


Best 'non-hiker' hike.

The perfect option if you're new to Vancouver or just visting, and not an exactly avid hiker. It's a short hike with minimal incline but stunning, panorama views (like a little mountaintop, without having to hike all the way to the top of a mountain).

Since it is only a short ways up the mountain, it doesn't get much (or any) snow and it remains hikeable year round. If you want to fall in love with Vancouver, or make other people fall in love with Vancouver, take them on this hike. Additionally, aside from the hike, if you have never been to Deep Cove, the city alone is a lovely, small town with beautiful views even from the ground.
