About

What is this?

I moved here to Vancouver for school, which meant more time of my time was spent in libraries then the mountains. But the mountains are so close, I can't take it anymore. I want to do as many hikes as possible while I am here.

So, I created a list of everything I want to hike here while I have the chance and this is where I am keeping track.

Why I made this?

  • Vancouver is beautiful
  • The mountains deserve to be explored
  • It gets me off the couch
  • Nature is supposed to be good for humans
  • Fresh air, burning quads, exhausted lungs, sweat, sunrises/sunsets and mountain top views
